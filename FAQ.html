PetFinder
  • FAQ

  • Where/How do I create an account?

    You create an account on the home page under the login section. There is a link under the login boxes which allows you to make a new account. After the link is clicked a form opens asking for your first name, last name, email, and address to be submitted into our database.

  • Where/How do I add a pet to be tracked?

    You can add a pet from the profile page.

  • Where/How can I track my pet?

    Once your pet has been registered, you click the track button and it will show the pet's locations every two hours for the last twenty-four hours.

  • I found a pet, where can I input the dog collar number?

    You can input the dog collar on the home page at the bottom. Once the ID number is entered, the owner's information will pop up under the box you inputted the unique pet ID.
